Online Delivery and Menu

Feel free to browse through the cruisines, from local to international, served in our humble abode.
You may select the check-boxes, and click on the "Search" button, to speed up your search. For online delivery, under the food item, key in the quantity and preferences in the remarks column. Once you have complete ordering, click on the "Add to Cart" button below. After which, you may proceed to view the items in your carts and make payment by clicking on the "View Cart/Pay" button.
