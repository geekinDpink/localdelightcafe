About Local Delight Cafe

From its humble beginnings as a modest coffee stall in the 1980s, the name of Local Delight Cafe has become synonymous with unparalleled local delights and irresistibly fragrant coffee and tea, enjoyed amidst a cosy atmosphere. Today, Local Delights continues to delight fans and to attract converts.


Vision

To establish Local Delight Cafe as a household name in Singapore and Asia, offering delectable local cruisine and other complementary traditional dessert and beverages to one and all.


Mission

Local Delights strive to preserve its unique & rich heritage and pursue its excellence in its food and customer service.
